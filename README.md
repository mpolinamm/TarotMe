# TarotMe

**TarotMe** is a Flask-based web app that draws 3 random tarot cards and uses AI to generate personalized explanations of the reading. The site features an interactive card spread with visual representations of tarot cards (face-down before selection, face-up when revealed).

**TarotMe** lets you explore tarot readings in a fun and interactive way. Just draw three cards, flip them over, and get an AI-generated explanation of what your cards might mean for you. This app gives you the chance to ask about the questions that matter to you, receive a daily prediction, or simply enjoy exploring the world of tarot.
